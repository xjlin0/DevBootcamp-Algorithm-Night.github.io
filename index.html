No Algorithm Night on November 25th

Information

Wednesday nights at DBC, current students and alumni get together to discuss a computer science topic, that occurs on interviews. Either for general knowledge or interview prep, each gathering lasts about an hour and a half and consists of:

  • 6:30 - 7:00pm: Pizza + Lecture
  • 7:00 - 7:45pm: 3 x 15 minute whiteboarding questions on the night's topic
  • 7:45 - 8:00pm: Review solutions for each question
